First ever Augmented and Virtual Reality UMD Hackathon. Learn, create, and share
all kinds of things in virtual or augmented reality. There will be food, prizes and more!
We look forward to seeing some awesome creations!

will have prizes from Unity, Unreal, Oculus and Microsoft. Unity will be giving
out pro licenses, Unreal will be giving their own prize and swag to the winners.
Oculus is being awesome and giving 4 VR Headsets to the winner(s) of the
hackathon! Finally, Microsoft is going to be giving away Dell Venue Pro 8 and 2
Microsoft Bands. In addition to the prizes, we will have a lot of opportunities
to learn more VR | AR skills with workshops and fun events such as Smash
tournaments and outside events.
